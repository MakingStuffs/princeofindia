Our Menu

We have a menu consisting of carefully selected traditional dishes which originate from North and South India. We have a wide range of poultry, lamb, fish and vegetarian curries, and do not serve any pork or beef.

The exotic dishes on our menu include a mix of secret family recipes, which have been passed through multiple generations, and homeland comforts.

Our Bar

No true Indian restaurant is complete without a fully equipped bar to match. And we are no different.

Our bar is fully licensed and is happy to provide you with a wide range of beers (on tap and bottled), wines, spirits, juices and mixers.

We also serve traditional Indian drinks such as Lassi (an Indian milkshake) and chai (tea), as well as bar snacks.

Takeout

Takeout

If you would to eat your food in the comfort of your own home, that's no problem! We provide a full traditional Indian takeout service covering the local Burlington area.

You can choose to have your food delivered by one of our takeout providers -- Skip The Dishes, Door Dash -- or collect it from our restaurant yourself -- we currently offer a discount on collections.

Catering

Catering

We are able to provide a traditional Indian catering service for any of your special occasions.

Our catering service will enable you to serve all your favorite Indian dishes to your guests, with the same high standard you would expect in our restaurant.

If you would like to bring the traditional flavours of our Indian cuisine to your event please contact us using our contact form.

Dining

Dining

Our traditional Indian restaurant serves a wide variety of freshly prepared and traditional North and South Indian dishes.

All dishes have been carefully selected using a mix of secret family recipes and national favorites.

We provide our guests with a truly traditional experience and cater to all allergies and we do not keep, or serve, any pork or beef on our premises.
