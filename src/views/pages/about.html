Family Run

Prince of India is a family run establishment which has been serving traditional Indian cuisine for over two decades.

Our family started in Batala, Punjab, and now spans across three continents (America, Asia, Europe).

Our priority is, and always has been, to provide the world with tasty and healthy food which encapsulates our proud heritage.

All food served in our restaurants is pork and beef free, and combines a mix of secret family recipes with traditional Indian favourites.

International

We are proud to currently be operating out of three continents.

We have restaurants in Germany (Frankfurt, Stuttgart, Mannheim), India (Batala) and Canada (Ontario).

All of our branches provide the same high quality standard of traditional Indian food, mixed with our own secret family recipes.
